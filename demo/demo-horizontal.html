SnapPoint.js Demo

Easy-to-use jQuery plugin that "SnapPoints." A SnapPoint is the beginning of a block of content that you want the browser's window to scroll down to to. Metaphorically stated, SnapPoint attaches a little vacuum to any block level element (e.g. <div>). It sucks the visitors screen down to the top of that content, or if paging is horizontally it sucks the content to the left of the screen.
